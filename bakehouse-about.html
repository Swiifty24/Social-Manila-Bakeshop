Our Story

The Social Manila Bakehouse

Welcome to The Social Manila Bakehouse. Born from a passion for authentic, artisanal craftsmanship and the simple joy of sharing a perfect bite, we are dedicated to bringing you an elevated bakery experience. Every pastry is meticulously handcrafted in our bustling kitchens using only the finest, carefully sourced ingredients.

Whether you are here to savor our signature butter croissants, create your own masterpiece in our Co-Baking Studio, or simply enjoy a quiet moment with a freshly poured coffee, we strive to make every visit memorable. We believe that great food is the foundation of a vibrant community.

The Studio Experience

A glimpse into our collaborative baking space, artisanal creations, and the vibrant community that brings The Social Manila Bakehouse to life.

The Social Manila Network

The bakehouse is just one part of the broader vision of The Social Manila. We are a collective dedicated to nurturing the entrepreneurial spirit of our community and providing spaces where collaboration, art, and lifestyle meet.

Through our sister companies, we offer a comprehensive ecosystem for living, working, and learning. From creative coworking spaces to professional culinary education, explore the rest of The Social Manila lifestyle.
